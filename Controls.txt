Controls:
0 - turns off all parallax and normal mapping
1 - turns on normal mapping
2 - turns on parallax mapping
3 - turns on parallax occlusion mapping
4 - turns on self shadowing occlusion mapping

wasd - Move camera
left ctrl - toggles wether the mouse is locked to the screen
mouse - rotates camera when it is locked

ijkl - moves the light (it is denoted by the smaller cube)

arrow keys - moves the cube

q - toggles rotation on the cube

e - toggles blur

8 - increases blur
9 - reduces blur
